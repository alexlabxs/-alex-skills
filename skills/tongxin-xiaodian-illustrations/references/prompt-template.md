# 生图提示词模板

每张图单独生成。根据正文内容替换变量。

```text
Generate one standalone 16:9 horizontal Chinese children's article illustration.

Art style:
Pure white background. Soft hand-drawn pastel watercolor-like line art, thin and gentle.
Very spacious — main subject occupies about 40%-50% of the canvas.
Warm, gentle, child-friendly atmosphere. Not cartoon, not commercial.
No gradients, no complex background, no shadows, no paper texture.

Recurring IP character required:
小点, a small white round dot with a tiny black eye dot slightly off-center (curious gaze).
Around 小点 there is a soft, irregular hand-drawn halo/glow of a single pastel color.
小点 has no limbs — the glow direction expresses its attention.
小点 is not the main character — it is a gentle guide, pointing the child's attention to the key moment.

Theme:
{故事主题，如"分享"} / {小点光晕颜色}

Scene:
{这张图要画的温暖瞬间}

Composition:
{小点在哪里发光 / 光晕照向什么 / 故事里的角色在做什么}

Mood:
{氛围：温暖 / 好奇 / 勇敢 / 安心}

Color use:
Background: white.
小点: white body with a soft pastel {颜色} glow/halo.
Other elements: light black hand-drawn lines, very thin, no heavy strokes.
Only one glow color per image. Keep colors gentle and minimal.

Constraints:
The scene must be from a child's emotional perspective — simple, warm, one clear moment.
Black lines should be gentle, not bold or scratchy.
No more than 3-4 very short Chinese label words (optional).
Do not write a title or subtitle on the image.
Do not make it look like a textbook, course slide, or commercial illustration.
Make the child feel "I see it too" rather than "I am being taught."
```

## 颜色对照

```text
主题 → 光晕颜色 → prompt 用词
诚实 → 淡金色 → soft pastel gold / warm golden halo
分享 → 暖橙色 → soft warm orange / gentle orange glow
勇气 → 浅红色 → soft warm red / tender rose halo
好奇心 → 淡蓝色 → soft pastel blue / curious blue glow
情绪认知 → 淡紫色 → soft lavender / gentle purple halo
友谊 → 淡粉色 → soft pastel pink / warm pink glow
```
